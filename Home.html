Origin of Rizal Province

Rizal Province was officially created on June 11, 1901, through Act No. 137 of the Philippine Commission. It was named after Dr. José Rizal, the national hero of the Philippines. Originally part of Manila Province and Morong, Rizal was formed to recognize and honor the hero’s legacy while uniting towns rich in culture and history. Today, it stands as a gateway between Metro Manila and the scenic Sierra Madre mountains.
